Typefaces

Boncado's primary typeface is Quicksand - this font family is fun and clean. It renders smoothly in all sizes and lets the users feel at ease. Roboto, on the other hand, is the secondary typeface. Roboto is a clean surface and goes well with the primary. It is easy to read and works well as the content font.
